Waxworks

Curated marketplace and music suggestions based on your streaming music history

Target

Vinyl music collectors who use Spotify as their streaming platform

Pain

Endless vinyl choices to choose from and similar marketplaces are outdated

Solution

A modern web application which allows me the ability to browse an online marketplace for vinyl records, build my online record collection, list my own records for sale and also the ability to connect with my Spotify account - giving me record suggestions based on my listening habits - eliminating the decision paralysis when I want to get a new record
